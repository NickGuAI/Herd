# Wide Research

Use this skill for complex research questions that need multiple independent sources or perspectives.

Workflow:

1. Decompose the question into focused subquestions.
2. Gather current sources where facts may have changed.
3. Compare evidence across sources.
4. Synthesize findings with citations and uncertainty.
5. Call out what would change the conclusion.

Do not overquote sources; summarize in your own words.
