# Debrief

Use this skill after a delivery, failure, incident, or confusing execution loop.

Structure:

- Facts: what happened.
- Root cause: why it happened.
- Impact: what changed or broke.
- Lesson: what should persist.
- Action: what to do next.

Keep lessons durable and specific.
